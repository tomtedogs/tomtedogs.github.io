Tomte Dogs is a leading dog-care service founded and run by Sarah Sanders. Specialising in a tailored and loving approach to their clients, Tomte Dogs offer a variety of hiking, walking and pastoral care services for the dogs of West and North West London.

Prior to founding Tomte Dogs in 2017, Sarah worked as a leading creative and producer in the music and entertainment industries. When her father died she took a step back from her hectic career and rediscovered the joy of caring for animals after adopting his terrier mutt Spike. Over the months that followed, and after increasing demand for her services from friends and like-minded pet owners, Sarah decided to turn her passion into a profession. Now, founded on the core principles of expert care, trust and reliability, Tomte Dogs operate a bespoke programme of daily activities for all manner of dogs across the capital.

With an ethos that prioritises physical and mental stimulation, sociability and (most importantly) fun, Tomte Dogs believe in treating every dog as if it were their own and keeping love at the heart of everything they do.
