Monday - Friday : Daily Hike Experience

Location : Hampstead Heath

Duration : 2 Hours

Suitable for medium to high energy dogs

Tomte Dogs operates a daily 2 hour hike Monday to Friday for up to 5 dogs. The hike takes place at Hampstead Heath and is a multi terrain experience involving a guided trek through woods and fields, and (weather permitting) swimming. Physical exercise and play is interspersed with affection. The aim is to give the dogs a fully rounded sensory experience in nature. Your dog will come home tired and happy from time spent romping in nature with the pack. Please note the duration does not include pick up and drop off time.

Cost = £40 per dog*

Catchment areas for pick ups: Queens Park, Little Venice, St Johns Wood, Belsize Park, Primrose Hill and Hampstead.

*£30 per dog for more than x 1 booking in the same week or for x 2 dogs from same household)

Monday - Friday : Evening Walk

Location : Holland Park

Duration : 45-60 minutes

Suitable for any dog requiring a light evening outing

Tomte Dogs operates a nightly dog walk in Holland Park between 6:30pm and 7:30pm. Working late and concerned for your canine companion waiting at home alone? Or perhaps you have to go out of an evening and need your dog to be given a light walk and some pastoral care, or have its supper set out? This walk is for you. Please note this walk wouldn’t be suitable as your dog’s only outing of the day.

Cost = £25 per dog

Catchment areas for pickups: Queens Park, Notting Hill, Holland Park and Kensington.

  • Participants in Tomte outings are transported to and from home in Sarah’s carefully modified vehicle which is designed for the dogs’ safety and comfort.

  • Tomte Dogs is fully insured. Sarah has a current DBS Certificate is a certified Animal First Aider.

  • Please contact Sarah for more details or to book a place for your dog.

  • Private and weekend walks are also available by special arrangement.
