Monday - Friday : Daily Dog Hike Experience

Tomte Dogs operates a daily 2 hour walk Monday to Friday for up to 5 dogs. The walk takes place at Hampstead Heath and is a multi terrain experience involving a guided hike through woods and fields, and (weather permitting) swimming. Physical exercise and play is interspersed with affection. The aim is to give the dogs a fully rounded sensory experience in nature. Your dog will come home tired and happy from day spent romping in nature with the pack. Photos and videos from the walk are posted to Instagram so you can see how much fun your dog has had that day.

Cost = £ per dog

Monday - Friday : Evening Walk (45 – 60 minutes)

Tomte Dogs operates a nightly dog walk in Holland Park between 6:30pm and 7:30pm Working late and concerned for your canine companion waiting at home alone? Or perhaps you have to go out of an evening and need your dog to be given a light walk and some pastoral care, or have its supper set out? This walk is for you. Please note this walk wouldn’t be suitable as your dog’s only outing of the day.

Cost = £25 per dog
